FAQ

How do I enter?

Buy $POT on Flap and hold at least 20,000 tokens on Robinhood Chain. You're automatically entered — no signup.

Why Flap + Robinhood Chain?

Flap handles the tax token launch and vault routing. Robinhood Chain is where the lottery runs — EVM-compatible, ETH gas, Robinhood Wallet support.

How do taxes work?

5% on buys and sells. 100% of tax goes to the vault via Flap's vault system — that's the prize pool for draws.

How often are draws?

Every 20 minutes. The full vault balance goes to one random eligible wallet.

Can I win more than once?

Yes. Keep holding above the minimum and you're in every single draw.
